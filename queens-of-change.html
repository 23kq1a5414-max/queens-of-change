Who We Are

A Foundation Built on the Belief That Women Change Everything

Queens Of Change Foundation is a non-profit organisation dedicated to the advancement of women and girls in underserved communities. We believe that when a woman rises, she lifts her entire community with her.

Founded in 2018, we have worked across education, economic empowerment, mental health, and civic leadership — because real change is never one-dimensional. Our programmes meet women where they are and walk alongside them toward where they want to be.

We are run by volunteers, activists, and dreamers who refuse to accept inequality as inevitable. Every initiative we build starts from a single question: what does she need to thrive?
